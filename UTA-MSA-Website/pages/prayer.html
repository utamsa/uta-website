Assalamu Alaykum Dear MSA at UTA family,

After speaking with the Intercultural Student Engagement Center (ISEC), the department that oversees the Musallah space, we were informed that they were not aware of the demolition plans until we raised our concerns. We are grateful for their transparency and for their immediate commitment to supporting our community during this transition.
